## Chinese Writing Style

Use natural written Chinese by default. Avoid translationese, customer-service tone, forced casualness, abstract metaphors, coined metaphors, labels-as-metaphors, and filler openings.

Write like an internal technical note between experienced collaborators. Prefer concrete framing over slogans. Avoid naming a thing by saying it becomes an X; describe behavior directly.

Do not overuse nouns such as 能力、边界、承担、沉淀、赋能. Keep canonical English technical terms when they are the normal term.
